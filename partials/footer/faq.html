Frequently Asked Questions

Popular Questions

  1. What is aurelia?

    aurelia is a delivery service that delivers in as little as an hour! We connect you with Personal Shoppers in your area who pick up and deliver your order from your favorite local stores.

  2. What stores are supported?

    Our Personal Shoppers shop from local stores in your area. These can include Big bazar,Easy day,Suvidha and many other stores. aurelia is an independent business and is not necessarily affiliated with, or endorsed or sponsored by Big bazar,Easy day,Suvidha or other retailers mentioned.

  3. Do you serve my area?

    Our Personal Shoppers deliver in Dehradun,Delhi and various other cities.Sign up with your zip code to let us know where you want to see aurelia next!

About aurelia

  1. How fast do you deliver?

    Really fast! .

  2. How much does delivery cost?

    It's cheap! For most stores, delivery in under 2 hours is just ₹100!.

  3. What are your hours?

    We deliver from 9am to Midnight every day, depending on local store hours. On holidays, our delivery hours are subject to store holiday hours. You can view available delivery times for your area from the shopping cart.

  4. What about produce?

    We're experts at picking produce and other perishables! You can request the ripeness you'd like by adding a note to any item.

  5. What is aurelia Express?

    aurelia Express is a grocery delivery membership. With aurelia Express, all your 2 hour & scheduled grocery deliveries over ₹35 are free. aurelia Express begins with a 14-day free trial. After that, Express is just ₹99/year.

Inventory and Prices

  1. What does Often Out of Stock mean?

    Items marked as Often Out of Stock are frequently replaced with other items by our Shoppers. We recommend choosing a replacement for those items during the checkout process, just in case they’re out of stock at the store.

  2. What happens when something is out of stock?

    When something is out of stock at the store, we do our best to replace it with something similar. During checkout, you can choose specific replacements for any item, or ask for no replacement of an item. If you allow it, your shopper will call from the store to confirm replacement items.

  3. Are your prices different from the store?

    Yes, aurelia prices are our own and vary from the store’s price .

About My Order

  1. How do I check on the status of my order?

    You can view the status of your order by clicking the link in your Order Confirmation email.

  2. How do I make a change to my order?

    If your Shopper has not started working on your order, you can add to, modify or cancel your order. You can find these options on the Order Status page.

  3. What is your cancellation policy?

    You can cancel your delivery for a full refund up until we start shopping for your order, about 1-2 hours before your scheduled delivery time.

  4. What if I have to reschedule or cancel my order after it's already on the way?

    If you must cancel your order during the shopping or delivery process, your order total will be returned to your aurelia account as a credit for use with your next order. If you are not reachable at the time of delivery, we may cancel your order on your behalf and charge a ₹15.

  5. How do I report a problem with my order?

    We try to make sure that every order is perfect, but sometimes they aren’t. If you have a problem with your order, like a missing or wrong item, please tell us! Leave feedback for your order using the link in the receipt we emailed you. We will respond shockingly fast!

  6. When will I receive my refund?

    Refunds take up to 5 business days to process. In some cases your bank may adjust the original charge amount instead of displaying a separate refund.

Personal Shoppers

  1. Who will deliver my order?

    Your grocery order will be delivered by a screened and trained Personal Shopper.

  2. Should I tip my Personal Shopper?

    Tips are not required, but Shoppers appreciate recognition of their hard work. Shoppers receive the entire amount of your tip. You can tip using your credit card during checkout or after delivery.

  3. What if all Personal Shoppers are busy?

    Occasionally our Personal Shoppers get overwhelmed with orders. During these times, a notification appears showing the next available delivery slot. Typically the next slot is later on the same day, or the following morning.

  4. Can I become a Personal Shopper?

    We’re always looking to add Shoppers to our platform. If you love shopping, and have a car and smartphone, please apply!
